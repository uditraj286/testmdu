Join Our Teaching Team

Inspire the next generation at Gyan Niketan

Why Teach at Gyan Niketan?

  • Positive, inclusive, and collaborative school culture
  • Ongoing professional development and growth opportunities
  • Comprehensive benefits supporting work-life balance

Current Opportunity: School Teacher

Key Responsibilities

  • Create engaging course material for students
  • Conduct interactive learning sessions
  • Assess student work and provide constructive feedback
  • Track student progress and attendance meticulously
  • Collaborate regularly with parents and educators
  • Organize and actively participate in co-curricular activities

Required Qualifications & Skills

  • Graduate with D.El.Ed or equivalent qualification
  • Minimum one year teaching experience preferred
  • Strong planning and communication skills
  • Passion for educating and nurturing students
  • Proficiency in English and Hindi

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary packages
  • Modern facilities and teaching resources
  • Supportive administration and constructive feedback
  • Opportunities for leadership in extracurricular activities

How to Apply

  1. Submit your CV and cover letter via our online form or email.
  2. Attend an interview with our leadership team.
  3. Selected candidates will be invited for a demo class.
