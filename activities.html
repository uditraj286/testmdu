Celebration of Special Days

Culture & Values

Independence & Republic Day

Parades, cultural performances, and student-led speeches instilling patriotism and civic sense.

Teachers' & Children's Day

Heartfelt tributes, mentor-student role reversals, and joyful activities celebrating guidance and childhood.

Environment & Yoga Day

Tree plantation, cleanliness drives, and mass yoga sessions promoting wellness and responsibility.



Religious Festivals

Celebrations featuring traditional rituals, music, and dance, fostering a sense of community and spirituality.

Memorials

Heartfelt tributes honoring the contributions of individuals and groups to society.

Special Occasions

Celebrations for birthdays, anniversaries, and other significant events fostering joy and togetherness.

Gyan Niketan
