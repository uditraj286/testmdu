Your Home Away from Home

At Gyan Niketan Hostel, we provide more than just accommodation. We create a nurturing environment where students from diverse backgrounds come together to form lasting friendships, develop life skills, and excel academically.

Our modern hostel facilities are designed to provide comfort, security, and convenience, ensuring that every student feels at home while pursuing their educational goals.

Our Hostel Facilities

World-class accommodation and amenities designed for student comfort and success

Modern Dormitory Rooms

Spacious rooms with personal study desks, premium bedding, and secure lockers for every student's comfort and safety.

Nutritious Meals

Fresh, delicious vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals served at hygienic dining halls with flexible timings and nutritional balance.

24x7 Security & CCTV

Guarded campus with biometric entry, comprehensive CCTV monitoring, and round-the-clock security for complete safety and peace of mind.

Sports & Recreation

Badminton courts, football field, fitness area, and cultural programs to keep students healthy, engaged, and socially active.

Academic Support

Onsite tutors, study groups, computer lab, and mentorship from experienced faculty for comprehensive academic growth and support.

Medical Care

Access to basic medical facilities, first-aid services, doctor-on-call for emergencies, and comprehensive wellness programs for student health.
