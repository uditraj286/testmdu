About Us

Building tomorrow's leaders through excellence in education and character development

Excellence in Education

Gyan Niketan, established in 1986 by Padma Shree Acharya Kishore Kunal, IPS, and Mrs. Anita Kunal under the M.V.V. Nyas, was built on the vision of providing quality education in a nurturing environment to shape responsible, compassionate citizens. Affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi, its first batch appeared in 1990, earning recognition at the national level. Over the years, it has grown into a reputed, result-oriented institution with hostel facilities for students from Std. I to XII, a spacious multi-discipline playground, and an auditorium that encourages excellence through diverse curricular and co-curricular activities.

The school offers a well-equipped Senior Secondary section with a wide range of subjects in Science, Commerce, Humanities, and Vocational streams, guided by competent faculty. Gyan Niketan promotes holistic growth—academic, physical, emotional, and social—ensuring students explore their full potential. Its alumni association, GYANOBA, remains actively connected, contributing to the institution's continual development and upholding its legacy of excellence.

Vision & Mission

Leaders emerge from virtue

"Education is not just about acquiring knowledge; it's about transforming lives and building character. At Gyan Niketan, we believe in nurturing not just academic excellence, but also moral values, leadership qualities, and social responsibility."

When I founded Gyan Niketan in 1986, my vision was simple yet profound—to create an institution that would not only impart quality education but also shape responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

Today, as I look back at our journey, I am proud to see how this vision has become a reality. Our students have excelled academically and grown into compassionate leaders, innovative thinkers, and responsible global citizens.

The foundation of our success lies in our commitment to:
