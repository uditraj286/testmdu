From Our Desk

Inspiring words from our educational leaders who guide and shape the future of Gyan Niketan

Founder's Message

[Padma Shree Acharya Kishore Kunal]

"Education is not just about acquiring knowledge; it's about transforming lives and building character. At Gyan Niketan, we believe in nurturing not just academic excellence, but also moral values, leadership qualities, and social responsibility."

Founder & Visionary Leader
"
Administrator's Desk

[Mrs. Anita Kunal]

"It is a matter of great pride and pleasure that Gyan Niketan School making great strides and touching the pinnacle of glory not only in academics but all spheres of life proving true to the real spirit and aim of education. The success owes to the dedicated efforts of all the staff members and an optimistic attitude. The stress on all round development coupled with no dearth of much reach such heights and prepare on discipline and morality has formed the basis and guideline for the pupil’s pursuit of education. We are determined to more ached and add new feathers to our success in every forthcoming session."

Administrator
"
Director's Message

[Mr. Sayan Kunal]

"As Education is the process of acquiring knowledge, skills and values that enable individuals to grow, develop and reach their full potential, Gyan Niketan, with its vast experience of shaping the legacy of education is committed to providing a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, creativity and personal growth."

Academic Director
"
Principal's Message

[Mr. Shailendra Mohan Jha]

"Education is something which makes man self-reliant and selfless” this statement of the Rig Veda was the guiding principles behind establishment of this school in the year 1986. Being principal of Gyan Niketan, I feel proud and privileged to welcome and extend our warm greetings to all those who visit our website. Gyan Niketan is an institution where we are committed to fostering a safe, supportive, and academically challenging environment where every student is encouraged to grow, explore, and thrive. Our school is a learning abode where we provide contemporary education while preserving the legacy of our rich culture and heritage. This website is a window through which we keep you connected and informed about the curricular and co-curricular activities of the school.

This is important to mention here that this website has been designed and developed by the students of Std. X of our school — Udit, Aryan, Anant, and Aadyant

School Principal
"
Head Boy's Message

[Mayank Kumar]

"To my fellow students: I stand with you and for you. Whether it's your academic goals, your creative ideas, or your concerns—you matter. My role is to listen, to support, and to make sure your voices are heard. Together, we will work to make this year not just successful, but unforgettable. "

Head Boy & Student Leader

Gyan Niketan

To serve a very lagging and visible need of the city and its surrounding with an institution which, besides giving the children the best education, cares for all round development physical, mental and spiritual and inculcates in them a deep love for high moral values and due respect for all religion.
