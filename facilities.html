World-Class Facilities

Providing a nurturing and innovative environment for holistic growth and development

Smart Classrooms

Digitally equipped classrooms with interactive smart boards, projectors, and modern teaching aids for enhanced learning experiences.

Central Library

Extensive collection of books, journals, digital resources, and quiet study areas to foster a love for reading and research.

Science Laboratories

Well-equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs with modern equipment for hands-on practical learning and experiments.

Computer Laboratory

State-of-the-art computers with latest software for programming, digital literacy, and technology education.

Sports Complex

Comprehensive sports facilities including basketball court, football field, and indoor games for physical fitness and recreation.

Medical Room

Fully equipped medical facility with qualified staff for first aid, health checkups, and medical emergencies.

Art & Craft Studio

Creative spaces with art supplies and tools to nurture artistic talents and encourage creative expression.

Music Room

Soundproof music room with various instruments and audio equipment for music education and practice sessions.

Cafeteria

Hygienic and spacious dining area serving nutritious meals and snacks with a variety of healthy food options.

Robotics Lab

State-of-the-art robotics lab equipped with advanced tools and technologies for hands-on learning.

Hostel

Comfortable and secure accommodation with all necessary amenities for a pleasant stay.

Transportation

Safe and reliable transportation options for students commuting to and from school.

Additional Features

Supporting infrastructure that makes learning comfortable and efficient

High-Speed WiFi

Campus-wide internet connectivity

24/7 Security

CCTV surveillance & security guards

Parking

Ample parking space for visitors

Gyan Niketan

To serve a very lagging and visible need of the city and its surrounding with an institution which, besides giving the children the best education, cares for all round development physical, mental and spiritual and inculcates in them a deep love for high moral values and due respect for all religion.
